## Purpose
Identify profitable gaps in crowded markets without scraping or paid data.

## Inputs
- Platform (e.g., Etsy, Gumroad, Upwork)
- Niche (e.g., fitness, AI, parenting)

## Process
1. List the top 10 sellers in the niche.
2. Identify:
   - Missing features
   - Complaints in reviews
   - Price clustering gaps
3. Output 3 underserved angles.

## Output Example
Niche: AI Prompts  
Gap: Non-technical solopreneurs needing plain-English workflows.
